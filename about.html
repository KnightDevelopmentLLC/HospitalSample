Who We Are

The Hospital Built for 2050

Founded on the belief that medicine should evolve at the speed of human need — NexaCare stands at the intersection of biological science and quantum technology.

Our Mission

Democratising Advanced Healthcare

We believe every human being deserves access to the most advanced medical care on Earth. Our mission is to make AI-powered, precision medicine universally accessible — regardless of geography, language, or economic status.


NexaCare's intelligent systems are deployed across 47 countries, serving patients in 28 languages through holographic, in-person, and nano-sensor-connected care pathways.

Our Vision

A World Without Preventable Disease

By 2060, NexaCare aims to eliminate the top 12 preventable disease categories through continuous AI monitoring, early genomic intervention, and our global health prediction network.


We envision a future where your health profile is monitored in real-time and conditions are caught — and corrected — before they ever become symptomatic.

Our Journey

25 Years of Breakthrough

2025 — Founding

NexaCare Medical Systems Established

Founded by Dr. Yuki Chen and a team of 12 biomedical AI researchers with the goal of creating the world's first AI-native hospital system.

2028 — Breakthrough

First AI Diagnostic Engine Goes Live

Our proprietary Nexa-DX diagnostic AI achieved 97.3% accuracy across 340 disease categories — surpassing specialist-level diagnosis for the first time.

2033 — Expansion

Global Network Launched Across 20 Countries

NexaCare's holographic consultation platform went live, enabling instant specialist access for patients in remote and underserved regions worldwide.

2038 — Innovation

Nano Therapeutics Division Opens

The world's first clinical-grade nano-drug delivery platform entered patient trials, achieving 99.1% targeted delivery accuracy with near-zero side effects.

2045 — Milestone

100,000 Patients Milestone & WHO Partnership

NexaCare treated its 100,000th patient and signed a landmark partnership with the WHO to deploy AI diagnostics in 150 developing nations.

2050 — Today

NexaCare Neo City Flagship Opens

The most advanced hospital facility ever constructed opens, featuring quantum-encrypted records, robotic surgery theatres, and real-time genomic sequencing for every patient.
